FAQs

What ages do you serve?

We serve children from 12 months (walking) up to 6 years old (Kindergarten), including our dedicated Toddler and Primary programs.

Can I bring my child to the tour?

Yes! We encourage you to bring your child so we can see how they interact with the Montessori environment.

Do children need to be potty trained?

Not for our Toddler program! We assist with potty training. For the Primary (3-6) program, potty training is usually required, but we can discuss specifics during your tour.

Do you offer tours on weekends?

Yes, we understand working parents have busy schedules. We offer select Sunday tour slots for your convenience.

Do you provide meals?

We provide healthy morning and afternoon snacks. To ensure dietary needs are met, parents provide a packed lunch.

Where are you located?

We are located at 751 E Union Hills Dr, Suite 19, Phoenix, AZ 85024.

Do you accept DES?

Yes, we accept Department of Economic Security (DES) assistance.

What are your hours?

We are open from 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday.

What is your teacher-to-student ratio?

We maintain low ratios to ensure personal attention. This allows our teachers to guide each child individually through the Montessori curriculum.

Do you offer part-time schedules?

Yes! We offer both full-time and part-time enrollment options to provide flexibility for your family's schedule.
