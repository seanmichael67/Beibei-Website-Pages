A Vision for Global Citizens

Meet Our Founder: Sean Michael Diana

Sean Michael Diana founded Beibei Amigos with a singular mission: to provide a rich, multilingual environment where children develop not just language skills, but a deep appreciation for global cultures.

With over 18 years of experience in early childhood education, Sean believes that the combination of Montessori principles and full language immersion creates the most powerful foundation for a child's future academic success.

Tuition & Financial Aid FAQs

When is tuition due?
Tuition is payable on a monthly basis.
How do I pay tuition?
We use the Procare app for all billing. It provides a convenient and secure way for parents to manage and pay tuition directly from their phones.
Do you offer drop-in rates?
No. To maintain a consistent immersion environment and routine for our students, Beibei Amigos does not offer drop-in care.
How much of the tuition is covered by DES?
The amount covered by DES depends on each family's income and eligibility. Please note that parents are responsible for the remaining tuition balance (the co-pay) that is not covered by the DES reimbursement.
Do you have Quality First Scholarships available?
Yes, we are a Quality First participant and have four scholarships allocated to our school. However, these are subject to availability. Please contact us directly to check if a scholarship spot is currently open.
