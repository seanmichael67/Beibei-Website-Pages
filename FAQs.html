General Information

What are your hours?
Beibei Amigos is open Monday through Friday. Program schedules may vary by classroom and age group. Please contact the school for current start and end times to see what fits your needs.
Where is the school located?
We are located at 751 E. Union Hills Drive, Phoenix, AZ 85024 (North Phoenix).
What is your phone number?
You can reach our main office at 602-996-4990.
How long has Beibei Amigos been open?
Beibei Amigos has been proudly serving families in the Valley since 2009.
Are you licensed?
Yes, we are a fully licensed preschool regulated by the Arizona Department of Health Services (ADHS) and adhere to all state safety and educational standards.
What is the sign-in/sign-out policy?
A parent or authorized adult must sign the child in and out daily using our digital system. This is a state requirement for safety and ratio tracking. If you forget to sign in/out, a missing signature fee of $15 may apply.
Are there fees for late pick-up or early drop-off?
Yes. Non-contracted early drop-off or late pick-up (without notice) incurs a fee of $15 for the first 15 minutes and $5 for every minute thereafter to compensate staff.
Do you take photos of the children?
Yes, we take photos for classroom use, the Procare app, and children's portfolios. With permission, photos may also be used for our website or social media to celebrate school activities.
Are you a franchise?
No. Beibei Amigos is a private, locally owned early childhood education school. We are not a franchise.
Who founded the school?
Beibei Amigos was founded by Sean Michael Diana, a former elementary teacher and administrator. Inspired by his time living in Spain and becoming fluent in Spanish, he opened Beibei Amigos to provide high-quality immersion education for his own son and the community.
Do you have a family handbook?
Yes, all enrolled families receive a comprehensive Family Handbook detailing our policies, procedures, and helpful information for parents.
What holidays are you closed?
We are closed for major national holidays. A specific school calendar with all closure dates is provided to families upon enrollment.
Do you have an elementary school?
Yes! Our sister school, Arizona Language Preparatory (ALP), continues the immersion journey through elementary school.
Location: 6140 E Thunderbird Rd., Scottsdale, AZ 85254
Website: azlanguageprep.org

Enrollment & Tuition

What ages do you accept?
We serve children from 12 months (walking toddlers) through 5 years old (Pre-K).
Can my child enroll mid-year?
Yes! We accept enrollment year-round, provided there is space available in the specific age group/classroom.
Do you offer trial days?
Yes, trial or transition days can be arranged to help ensure a smooth adjustment for your child before their official start date.
Do you have a summer camp?
Yes, we offer a fun and engaging summer program to keep language skills sharp and children active during the summer months.
Do you have an after-school program?
Availability for after-school programs may vary. Please contact the office for the most current information regarding extended care options.
Do you offer part-time or half-day options?
Yes. Enrollment options range from three half-days per week up to five full days per week to suit your family's schedule.
How much is tuition and how do I pay?
Tuition varies based on the age of the child and the schedule chosen. Payments are made monthly via the Procare app. We can arrange weekly payment plans upon request. View our tuition page for more details.
Are there other fees besides tuition?
Yes. There is a one-time non-refundable Registration Fee of $300 at enrollment. There is also a quarterly Supply Fee of $70 (billed Jan, Apr, July, Oct) to cover classroom materials.
Do you offer a sibling discount?
Yes! Families receive a 10% discount on the lowest tuition rate when enrolling more than one child.
Is there a fee for credit card payments?
Yes, credit/debit card transactions incur a 3.5% processing fee. We recommend using ACH (bank transfer), check, or cash to avoid this fee.
Do you accept DES assistance?
Yes, we are an approved provider for DES childcare assistance.
Do you accept ESA?
Please contact our office directly to discuss ESA funding and current scholarship program availability.
Is there a military discount?
Yes, we offer discounts for military families. Thank you for your service!
Is there a discount for teachers?
Yes, we offer special discounts for fellow educators.
Do you offer vacation weeks?
No, we do not offer credit for vacation weeks. Tuition holds your child's spot in the class.
What is the withdrawal policy?
A written notice must be provided at least 2 weeks prior to withdrawal. Families are responsible for tuition during this notice period.
Can I visit the school first?
Absolutely. We strongly encourage you to schedule a tour. It's the best way to meet the teachers and see if Beibei Amigos is the right fit for your family.

Daily Life

Do children take naps?
Yes. All full-day children have a supervised rest/nap time. Naps take place inside the classroom on individual mats in a calm, quiet environment.
Does my child need to be potty trained?
Children entering the 3-year-old classroom must be fully potty trained (able to use the toilet independently). For younger children, we partner with families on toilet learning when the child shows signs of readiness.
Do I need to provide diapers?
Yes. Parents of children in diapers must provide diapers and wipes (labeled with the child's name). If a child runs out, the school will provide them for a fee ($3/diaper, $5/wipes pack) after notification.
Can my child bring toys from home?
Generally, no. We ask that personal toys stay at home to prevent loss or conflict. Exceptions may be made for "Show and Tell" days or a small soft comfort item for nap time (no hard plastic parts).
How often do children go outside?
Weather permitting, children enjoy multiple outdoor recess times daily to play, run, and develop gross motor skills.
Do the bathrooms have small toilets?
Yes, our facilities are designed for young children and are equipped with age-appropriate, child-sized toilets and sinks to encourage independence.
Do you have performances?
Yes! We host cultural and holiday performances where children sing songs and perform in Spanish and Mandarin, giving them a chance to showcase their language skills.
Do you have Parents' Night Out?
We occasionally offer Parents' Night Out events where children can enjoy fun activities at the school while parents have an evening off. Check our calendar for dates!
Is lunch provided?
No, parents provide lunch. Please pack a healthy lunch and a water bottle for your child each day.
Do you provide snacks?
Yes, the school provides healthy snacks during the day.
What supplies should I bring?
Generally, you will need to bring a lunch, water bottle, change of clothes, and bedding for nap time (if full day). A specific supply list for your child's age group will be provided upon enrollment.

Health & Safety

Can you administer medication?
Staff can only administer medication for chronic conditions (like asthma/epi-pens) with a care plan. We do not apply over-the-counter items like sunscreen, lotion, or bug spray; please apply these before school.
Are immunizations required?
Yes, up-to-date immunization records are required for enrollment. We follow the ADHS schedule. Exemptions are accepted with proper legal documentation, though unimmunized children may be excluded during outbreaks.
Do you do health checks?
Yes, we conduct a quick visual health check upon arrival each day to ensure every child is well enough to participate and to protect the health of the group.
Do you use pest control?
Yes, for safety and sanitation, licensed professionals treat the facility as needed outside of school hours. We use child-safe products and parents are notified 72 hours in advance of routine treatments.
How do you handle food allergies?
We are a nut-free campus. For other allergies, we work closely with parents to document medical needs and ensure all staff are aware of safety protocols.
What is the discipline policy?
We practice positive guidance and redirection. Our goal is to teach social-emotional skills, helping children understand feelings and resolve conflicts constructively rather than using punishment.
What do you do when a child bites?
Biting can be a developmental phase for toddlers. We address it by comforting the bitten child and firmly but gently redirecting the biter. We work closely with parents to identify triggers and prevent recurrence.
What are the teacher ratios?
We strictly adhere to or exceed the state-mandated teacher-to-child ratios for every age group to ensure safety and individual attention.
What is your security policy?
Student safety is our top priority. We utilize a coded door entry system and have strict procedures for supervised drop-off and pick-up to ensure only authorized persons can access the facility.
What if my child gets sick at school?
If a child becomes ill during the day, we will contact parents immediately. We ask that you pick up your child promptly to maintain a healthy environment for all students.

Curriculum & Staff

What is the curriculum?
We offer families a choice between two distinct learning tracks: Montessori (child-led, hands-on) or Traditional (play-based, teacher-led). Both programs integrate full Spanish and Mandarin immersion with art, music, math, and literacy concepts.
What is language immersion?
Immersion means that the foreign language (Spanish or Mandarin) is the medium of instruction, not just a subject. Teachers speak to the children in the target language throughout the day, helping them learn it as naturally as they learn their first language.
What is Montessori?
Our Montessori track follows the philosophy of Maria Montessori, emphasizing child-led activities, independence, and hands-on learning materials, all conducted within our language immersion environment.
What if my child starts speaking another language to me?
This is a great sign! It means the immersion is working. Even if you don't speak the language, encourage them. Let them "teach" you words—it boosts their confidence and reinforces their learning.
What certifications do your teachers have?
Our teaching staff is highly qualified. Montessori teachers are fully Montessori-certified. Traditional classroom teachers hold CDA (Child Development Associate) credentials or ECE (Early Childhood Education) degrees.
How is the teacher retention?
We are proud of our low turnover. Many of our Mandarin teachers have been with Beibei Amigos for over 7 years, providing a stable and loving environment for the children.
How many language teachers do you have?
We currently have 14 dedicated Spanish and Mandarin teachers, in addition to support staff.
How does the language immersion work?
Children are immersed in Spanish, Mandarin, and English throughout the day. Teachers use the target languages during play, circle time, songs, and instruction, helping children acquire language naturally.
Does my child need to know Spanish or Mandarin to enroll?
Not at all! No prior language experience is required. Our program is designed to teach children regardless of their home language.
Can I choose just one language (e.g., only Spanish)?
No. Our unique trilingual model is designed to expose all children to Spanish, Mandarin, and English. We believe this broad exposure is best for brain development.
Do the teachers teach English?
Yes, English is part of the curriculum to ensure children are prepared for Kindergarten standards, but the primary focus of our unique program is on Spanish and Mandarin acquisition.
How will I know how my child is doing?
We use the Procare app to send daily updates, photos, and messages. You can also communicate via email or phone, and we are always happy to discuss your child's progress.
Are there parent-teacher conferences?
Yes, we schedule conferences to discuss development milestones and answer any specific questions you have about your child's learning.
Is this a play-based or academic program?
It is a balance of both. We prepare children for Kindergarten by building foundations in literacy, math, and social skills (sharing, independence, problem-solving), all while they learn new languages.
Why choose Beibei Amigos?
We offer a rare combination: true trilingual immersion (Spanish/Mandarin/English), flexible schedules, and a choice between Montessori or Traditional learning styles, all led by experienced educators who truly care.
My child has separation anxiety. How do you help?
This is very common! Our teachers are trained to handle separation anxiety with patience and love. We establish consistent drop-off routines and use distraction and engagement to help them feel safe and happy quickly.
