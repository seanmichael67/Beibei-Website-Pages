Common Questions from Local Families

I live in Anthem. Is the drive worth it?

Absolutely. Many of our families drive from Anthem (approx. 15 mins) specifically for our unique trilingual immersion program, which provides a cognitive advantage not found in standard local preschools.

Do you provide transportation from local schools?

We do not provide transportation. However, our central location near the Loop 101 and I-17 makes drop-off and pick-up convenient for parents commuting from Desert Ridge, Stetson Hills, and beyond.

Which elementary schools do your students transition to?

Our graduates successfully transition to top local schools including Stetson Hills, Fireside, Norterra Canyon, and Wildfire Elementary, often entering with advanced language and social skills.

My neighborhood isn't listed. Can I still enroll?

Yes! We welcome families from all over the Valley. If you can commute to our campus at Union Hills & 7th St, we would love to have you join our community.

Do you offer flexible schedules for working parents?

Yes, we offer full-day programs (7:00 AM – 6:00 PM) as well as part-time options (3, 4, or 5 days a week) to accommodate the diverse schedules of families in North Phoenix.

How do I verify if you serve my area?

If you live or work in zip codes 85024, 85050, 85083, 85085, 85086, or surrounding areas, you are well within our typical service range. Schedule a tour to see the campus.
