Welcome to Beibei Amigos, the only Trilingual Preschool Phoenix families choose for authentic language immersion. Beibei Amigos Trilingual Programs

Carefully structured programs that grow with your child, ensuring optimal language development at every stage.

✨ Traditional Program

Play-Based Learning

👥 Toddlers: 1 year (walking) to 2 years old | Preschool: 3 to 5 years old
🕐 Full and Half Days Available

Our traditional program uses play-based learning to naturally immerse children in Spanish and Mandarin while building essential skills through exploration and creativity. This Pre-K pathway focuses on early socialization and learning.

Program Highlights:
  • Spanish & Mandarin immersion through songs, stories, and games
  • Play-based curriculum that builds social, emotional, and academic skills
  • Hands-on projects that spark creativity and curiosity
  • Early literacy and language arts in three languages through play centers
  • Math concepts introduced with puzzles, manipulatives, and group activities
  • Cultural exploration with music, art, and dramatic play
  • Leadership opportunities through classroom jobs and collaborative play

🎓 Montessori Program

The Five Areas of Learning

👥 Toddlers: 1 year (walking) to 2 years old | Preschool: 3 to 5 years old
🕐 Full and Half Days Available

Our Montessori program follows Maria Montessori's philosophy with full language immersion in Spanish, Mandarin, and English. Perfect for Kindergarten readiness.

Program Highlights:
  • Practical Life: Children build independence and confidence through self-care routines, food preparation, and care of the classroom environment
  • Sensorial: Materials develop the senses by exploring shapes, colors, textures, and patterns, forming the foundation for math and science
  • Language: Immersion in Spanish, Mandarin, and English with phonetic materials, vocabulary development, and storytelling
  • Mathematics: Hands-on Montessori math materials introduce counting, number sense, place value, and operations
  • Cultural Studies: Geography, science, and global awareness brought to life through maps, globes, nature studies, and cultural celebrations

The Neuroscience of Early Multilingualism

The human brain is most receptive to language acquisition between birth and age 7, a phase known as the critical period. During this window, high levels of neuroplasticity allow children to acquire Mandarin, Spanish, and English simultaneously, not just as subjects, but as native cognitive pathways.

Executive Function & Prefrontal Cortex: Navigating three linguistic systems strengthens the brain's command center, improving focus, impulse control, and multitasking abilities.
Cognitive Flexibility & Divergent Thinking: Multilingual children demonstrate superior pattern recognition and abstract problem-solving skills compared to monolingual peers.
Metalinguistic Awareness: Understanding the structure of multiple languages builds a stronger foundation for early literacy and reading readiness in English.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our programs and admissions

What ages do you accept?

We welcome toddlers (1 year walking to 2 years old) and preschoolers (3 to 5 years old). All programs feature full language immersion.

Is Beibei Amigos a Montessori school or a daycare?

More than just a daycare, we provide a comprehensive early childhood education. We offer both Montessori and Traditional curriculums with a focus on Kindergarten readiness.

What languages are taught?

We provide 100% language immersion in Spanish, Mandarin Chinese, and English—preparing children for a global future.

Do you offer part-time schedules?

Yes, families may select from 3 half days up to 5 full days per week. We partner with you to design a flexible schedule.

Do you accept DES child care assistance?

Yes, we are a DES-certified provider. We accept Department of Economic Security (DES) assistance to help eligible families with tuition costs.

Can I enroll my child in only Spanish or only Mandarin?

No, our program is a fully immersive trilingual experience. All students benefit from exposure to Spanish, Mandarin, and English to maximize cognitive development. We do not offer single-language tracks.
