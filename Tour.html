Enrollment FAQ

Is there a registration fee?

Yes, there is a non-refundable registration fee due upon enrollment to secure your child's placement in our Montessori or Traditional program.

Do you accept DES?

Yes! We are proud to accept Department of Economic Security (DES) child care assistance. Please use the DES button above to apply.

Is there a waitlist?

Spaces for our immersion program fill up quickly. We recommend submitting your form early, especially for the Fall semester.
