Our Vision for Global Citizens

Founded by Sean Michael Diana

Cave Creek and Carefree are known for their rural luxury, wide-open desert landscapes, and families who value independence and quality. Whether you're on an equestrian estate near Black Mountain or in the master-planned community of Tatum Ranch, you expect the very best for your children. Founder Sean Michael Diana created Beibei Amigos 18 years ago with a mission to provide the kind of rigorous, globally-minded education that Cave Creek families deserve.

As the premier Montessori option south of Carefree Highway, we prepare children for Cave Creek's outstanding immersion elementary programs—Horseshoe Trails, Lone Mountain, and Desert Willow. By introducing Mandarin, Spanish, and English during the brain's most plastic years, we equip them with the cultural empathy and cognitive tools needed to thrive.

Beibei Amigos Trilingual Programs

Carefully structured programs that grow with your child, ensuring optimal language development at every stage.

T

Traditional Program

Play-Based Learning

Toddlers: 1 year (walking) - 2 years

Preschool: 3 - 5 years

Full and Half Days Available

Our traditional program uses play-based learning to naturally immerse children in Spanish and Mandarin while building essential skills through exploration and creativity. This Pre-K pathway focuses on early socialization and learning.

Program Highlights:

  • Spanish & Mandarin immersion through songs, stories, and games
  • Play-based curriculum building social, emotional, and academic skills
  • Hands-on projects that spark creativity and curiosity
  • Early literacy and language arts in three languages through play centers
  • Math concepts introduced with puzzles, manipulatives, and group activities
  • Cultural exploration with music, art, and dramatic play
  • Leadership opportunities through classroom jobs and collaborative play
M

Montessori Program

The Five Areas of Learning

Toddlers: 1 year (walking) - 2 years

Preschool: 3 - 5 years

Full and Half Days Available

Our Montessori program follows Maria Montessori's philosophy with full language immersion in Spanish, Mandarin, and English. Perfect for Kindergarten readiness.

Program Highlights:

  • Practical Life: Children build independence and confidence through self-care routines, food preparation, and care of the classroom environment
  • Sensorial: Materials develop the senses by exploring shapes, colors, textures, and patterns, forming the foundation for math and science
  • Language: Immersion in Spanish, Mandarin, and English with phonetic materials, vocabulary development, and storytelling
  • Mathematics: Hands-on Montessori math materials introduce counting, number sense, place value, and operations
  • Cultural Studies: Geography, science, and global awareness brought to life through maps, globes, nature studies, and cultural celebrations
